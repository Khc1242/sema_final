Stories:


Duffy is the cute, loveable teddy bear handmade as a gift for Mickey by his sweetheart Minnie Mouse. Together, Duffy and Mickey travel the world sharing exciting adventures and making lifelong friends, including ShellieMay, Gelatoni, StellaLou and CookieAnn.


Characters:


Duffy

Mickey was getting ready to set sail on a long sea voyage,
and Minnie made him a special teddy bear
to take with him so he wouldn’t be lonely.
Mickey loved the bear and name him Duffy.
They visited all sorts of exciting places
and made a lot of friends along the way.

ShellieMay

Minnie was making a new friend for Duffy,
who was a sweet girl.
The first time they met, Duffy gave her a seashell pendant.
“Let’s be friends!” said Duffy.
Minnie then got an idea to name her ShellieMay.

Gelatoni

Duffy was taking a walk with Mickey
and accidentally plopped his gelato to the ground.
A grey tuxedo cat named Gelatoni showed up
and began painting with the melting gelato with his tail.
Duffy smiled and started to paint together.
And that’s how Duffy and Gelatoni met!

CookieAnn

Duffy first met CookieAnn
when he was about to enjoy some cotton candy.
He accidentally bumped into CookieAnn
who was carrying a huge stack of waffles and all their treats flew into the air!
Luckily, CookieAnn caught the ingredients
and turned them into a delicious cotton candy waffle!

StellaLou

StellaLou is a little lavender-colored bunny,
with cute ears, starry eyes, and
dreams of dancing on the Broadway stage.
Duffy and StellaLou first met on an American waterfront where Duffy,
seeing StellaLou stumbled mid-practice, called out to her in concern.
StellaLou then shared with Duffy her dream of becoming a dancer.
